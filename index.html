    Who We Are

    African Landscape is a family-run business with over two decades of dedicated service in the Pretoria region. Our journey began with a simple passion: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary, sustainable environments that thrive in the local climate.

    Our Philosophy

    We believe great landscaping is a blend of art and science. Our approach is modern and organic, focusing on water-wise solutions and indigenous plants that enhance the local ecology while providing a beautiful, professional, and tranquil space for you to enjoy. From detailed garden maintenance to large-scale commercial designs, our 20+ years of experience guarantee exceptional results and unwavering professionalism.

    Our Professional Services

    We offer a complete range of services to manage, design, and maintain properties for both residential and corporate clients.

    Regular Garden Maintenance

    Comprehensive weekly and bi-weekly packages including mowing, trimming, weeding, and seasonal fertilization.

    Efficient Garden Waste Removal

    Daily and weekly removal of all garden debris, ensuring your property remains tidy, compliant with local regulations, and immaculately clean after every service.

    Comprehensive Weed Control

    Targeted, sustainable programs for manual and chemical control of invasive species, protecting the health and aesthetics of your garden beds and lawn.

    Soil Enrichment & Fertilization

    Customized feeding programs that restore vital nutrients, balance soil pH, and promote deep root health for vibrant, resilient plant growth year-round.

    Pest & Disease Control

    Expert diagnosis and treatment of common garden pests and plant diseases using eco-friendly methods.

    Professional Tree Care & Removal

    Expert pruning, health checks, and safe removal services for large and small trees by certified arborists.
